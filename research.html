My research

Where is the room for responsibility in the production and deployment of AI technologies? (PhD research) | Supervisory team: Prof. Phyllis Illari and Prof. Jack Stilgoe
PhD, Science and Technology Studies
Sept 2020 - present
I am a PhD researcher interested in AI technologies and how responsibilities change when these technologies are deployed from the private sector to the public sector. My research is interdisciplinary, as I work at the intersection of Philosophy of Science, Sociology of Science, and Responsible Innovation. In order to consider different ways of enacting processes of responsibility when creating AI systems, I have chosen to have 2 case studies. One is a medical-AI company who have to comply with strict forms of regulation, and other is a technology company that draws from ethics, and builds AI systems. I am currently analysing the data from my semi-structured interviews.
